About Me

Hello! I'm Akash, a passionate Java Backend Developer with over 3 years of hands-on experience in building scalable and efficient backend systems.


I have honed my skills in Java development, leveraging frameworks like Spring Boot to develop RESTful APIs and microservices. I am adept at designing and implementing database solutions using technologies such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.


I thrive in dynamic environments where I can collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software solutions. My goal is to continuously expand my knowledge and expertise while contributing to impactful projects that make a difference.


I'm always open to new challenges and opportunities for growth, so feel free to reach out!
